Dimension 1 — Who entered the criteria?

Did the Hesed itself define its eligibility rules in ServiceNow, or did JDC Region set them centrally? Hesed-entered signals that the Hesed is taking ownership of the process.

Dimension 2 — Same as regional standard?

If a pilot Hesed's criteria are identical to the regional template, no strategic change has happened. Different = progress. Same = the pilot work has not started.
